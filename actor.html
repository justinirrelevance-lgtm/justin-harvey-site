Biography

Justin John Harvey is a South African actor based in Seoul — one of the very few foreign actors to have built a sustained, fluent career inside the Korean film and television industry. Since his debut in Ashfall (2019), which recorded over 8.25 million admissions at the Korean box office, Harvey has worked with Korea's most prominent directors.

Cast in The Witch: Part 2 (2022) by director Park Hoon-jung, he reunited with the director on The Childe (2023) and Disney+ series Tyrant (2024) — one of the most-streamed titles on Disney+ Korea. In 2024, he finished 4th on Netflix's Physical 100: Underground — the highest placement of any foreign national.

Upcoming in 2026: A Shop for Killers (Disney+), Portraits of Delusion (Disney+, dir. Han Jae-rim), and Resurrection Man (dir. Baek Jong-yeol).
