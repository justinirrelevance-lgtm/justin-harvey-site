Biography

Eight years.
Fluent Korean.

Justin John Harvey is a South African actor based in Seoul — one of the very few foreign actors to have built a sustained, fluent career inside the Korean film and television industry. Since his debut in Ashfall (2019), recording 8.25 million admissions, he has worked with Korea's most prominent directors across film, streaming, and television.

Cast across three productions by director Park Hoon-jung — The Witch: Part 2, The Childe, and Disney+ series Tyrant (#1 streamed in Korea). In 2024, he finished 4th on Netflix's Physical 100: Underground — the highest placement by any foreign national.
